                    Tax Compliance Services 

We provide technical advice and support on the compliance with existing and newly issued regulations on tax matters at different levels of Government. Some of the services we provide will cover the following: 
Company Income Tax
Under this category, we shall assist you to process your Company Income Tax Clearance Certificate with the Federal Inland Revenue Service [“FIRS”]. This involves the computation of the Capital Allowances due to your company and advising you on how to lessen your tax burden through proper tax planning. We shall also process the Letter of Agreement on the Audited Accounts with the FIRS. This involves getting the agreement of the FIRS on the assets, liabilities and all expenditure items in the audited accounts.
Transfer Pricing 
There are new regulations on Transfer Pricing which requires diligent professional advice in order to comply. The penalties from FIRS for non-compliance is heavy and it is better to avoid it. We are capable of offering you the necessary advice in this specialised area.
Personal Income Tax
We will offer our services to you in the area of Personal Income Tax. We will advise you on how to meet the provisions of the relevant tax laws in relation to remuneration and executive compensation package. We will do this through effective and efficient tax planning, accounting documentation and completion of tax forms as well as taking full advantage of the allowances due to them.
