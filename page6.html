Cash Protection Technology

We are Business Partners to Oberthur Cash Protection (“OCP”). OCP is a global leader in the application of Intelligent Bank Notes Neutralisation System (“IBNS”), otherwise known as Dye-Stained Banknotes Technology, in Africa, Europe, Americas and Asia. The very first ink staining system was invented in Dijon (France) in 1985, giving Oberthur Cash Protection an incomparable experience to draw on.

IBNS is used for:
  • the protection of ATMs from physical attacks: burglaries, robberies, ram raids and explosive.
  • the protection of Banknotes during Cash-in-Transit Operations.
  •  the protection of Retailers who deal in Cash .

We offer Interactive Touchscreen to our clients. The use of the Interactive Touchscreen is a great and remarkable way to optimize business performance. The Interactive Touchscreen enhances and transforms the way you work. It makes meetings and Training Programs more engaging and Interactive.   

                     Highlights and Benefits of Interactive Touchscreen




-       Ease of Use

        The Interactive Touchscreen makes your Presentation easy, unique and exciting. It enables         you to present information in a stimulating, clear, sharp and distinctive way at meetings. It         is simply an engaging and informative way to do Presentation.

-      Time Management at Meetings

        It enables you to manage time better and more efficiently at Meeting Rooms, multimedia         presentations and electronic whiteboard / flipchart are available at the touch of your         fingers. There is no need of manual white board/flipchart and also no need of projectors.         Key personnel or other participants can easily join the meeting as and when required from         different locations.  

-      Interactive Teleconferencing

        This Interactive Touchscreen technology enables you to interact with up to 100         participants with sound and visual clarity. And it allows you to connect with your own         device (Phone, PDA, Laptop, iPad) if necessary.

-      Transforms the Way you Do Training

         It empowers you to do interactive tele-training across multiple locations. It also allows         multimedia lesson delivery, such as videos, graphs, slides, etc.

-      Cost Savings

        The Interactive Touchscreen helps you to save costs. It has low maintenance cost. It last         longer than the Projector based Technologies. The service life is 30,000 hours compared         with 3,000 hours for the Projector based Technologies. As a Corporate Organization, you         can save money on travelling and accommodation as some of your meetings can be held         with the Interactive Touchscreen with sound and visual clarity and you can interact with         the participants. Also the funds for Training can be more efficiently managed since you         don’t have to travel to all your locations to conduct some of your Training Programs.

-      Environmentally Friendly

         It is environmentally friendly and customizable. There is no need for dimming of light or         closing of blinds like that of Projector based Technologies because the image brightness         remains constant. The room remains as bright as you want it to be. In addition, the system         is noiseless and runs silently unlike the noise from the fan of the Projector.

-      Equipment

        We obtain our Interactive Touchscreen from the original equipment manufacturer offering         technology- enhanced solutions, interactive tele-presence and digital signage solutions.         We offer one year Warranty on the Equipment.

The 3D Digital Imaging is the modern way of displaying of items for reading and learning purposes in a distinctive clear format. It’s a three-dimensional technique that captures and reproduces the shape and appearance of an object in picture format that is easy to view and understand. It is a technology that helps to preserve and store information, facts and shape of artefacts, artworks, precious metals, and Notes which are usually kept in Vaults, Depositories and Museums. The information and data captured in digital form is embedded in the Interactive Touchscreen where it can be easily accessed and displayed for viewing pleasure of clients, guests and visitors on the Interactive Touchscreen.

The 3D Digital Imaging production usually makes a deep and long-lasting impact on people than mere view of such items. Moreover the records can be easily preserved for posterity even if the physical/original item is misplaced, damaged or removed.
